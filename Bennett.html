“Hey! Would you mind stopping the car? I’m feeling dizzy. I need to take a breather.” “Yeah let me just pull up in this alley and you can step out,” says the driver.

He parks into the alleyway.

I get out coughing, trying to catch my breath. What’s happening? Why do I feel like this?

I lean back onto the car trying to pace myself. All of sudden I feel the car starting up and before I even had the chance to move. It pushes me to the ground, and I hit my head on the pavement, and feel the car go over me.

I scream, “HELP!”. I feel blood rushing out of my head. My body is in so much pain. I’m trying to scream help, but it feels like no one can hear me. I’m slowly losing consciousness. Everything is starting to get blurry. I’m moving my mouth, but the words don’t seem to be coming out. Why did they car run over me? Why did they leave me here? What was the reason? *Stares into sky*
